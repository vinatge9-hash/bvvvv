Our Story

Paw & Trust Dog Care started with a simple belief: every dog deserves compassionate, individualized attention when their humans can’t be there. Our team combines years of veterinary-inspired knowledge with playful, positive training to create a safe, happy environment for every canine.

Our Values

  • Compassionate care tailored to each dog’s needs
  • Transparent communication with owners
  • Positive reinforcement and safety-first approach
